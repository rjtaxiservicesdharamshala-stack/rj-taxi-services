FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our taxi services.

How do I book a taxi with RJ Taxi Services?
Booking is simple — call us at +91-9317082083, WhatsApp us, or use the booking form on this page. We confirm your booking within minutes and send all driver details before your travel date.
Do you offer airport pickup from Gaggal Airport (Kangra)?
Yes, we offer 24/7 pickup and drop service from Gaggal Airport (Kangra) to Dharamshala, McLeodganj, Palampur, and all surrounding areas. We track your flight so you're never left waiting.
What is included in your Himachal sightseeing packages?
Our sightseeing packages include an AC vehicle, professional driver, fuel, and driver allowance. Hotel stays and meals are not included but can be arranged on request. Entry fees and parking charges are payable at actuals.
Do you cover inter-state trips?
Absolutely! We operate across Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Delhi, Chandigarh, Jammu & Kashmir, Uttarakhand, and the broader North India region. All inter-state permits are handled by us.
What vehicles do you have for large groups?
We have Tempo Travellers (10-16 seats), Luxury AC Buses (20-45 seats), and Volvo Coaches for large group movements. We handle corporate trips, pilgrimages, school tours, and wedding transportation.
Is pricing fixed or metered?
We offer fixed, transparent pricing quoted upfront. There are no hidden charges or meter surprises. You agree to the price before the journey begins — that's our promise.
